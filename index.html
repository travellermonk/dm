Goa Trip Itinerary (16–19 Nov)

Chill, sightseeing, beaches — Le Seasons, Candolim.

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1 — 16 Nov (Arrival & Chill)

14:30 Arrive in Goa → Check-in at Le Seasons Resort (Candolim)

16:30 Candolim Beach → Sunset & relax at shacks

18:30 Walk the Candolim local market

20:00 Dinner — Fisherman’s Cove / Mermaid / De Candolim Deck / Grills N Curries

Day 2 — 17 Nov (North Goa + Arambol)

09:00 Start sightseeing

  • Aguada Fort + Lighthouse (optional)
  • Sinquerim Beach
  • Dolphin Trip (optional)
  • Calangute Beach → Baga Beach
  • Lunch (1:30 PM) at Britto’s / Infantaria / St. Anthony’s
  • Free time (shops / creek) around 3:30 PM
  • Chapora Fort → Vagator → Anjuna
  • 17:45 Arambol Beach — sunset, chill & photos
  • Return to Candolim (~19:30), Dinner near hotel
Day 3 — 18 Nov (South Goa Sightseeing)

10:00 Pickup → Mangeshi Temple → Basilica of Bom Jesus → St. Francis Xavier Church → Archaeological Museum

13:00 Lunch at Spice Plantation (own cost)

15:00 Explore Panjim: Church of Immaculate Conception, Latin Quarter (Fontainhas)

16:45 Viewpoint at Dona Paula

17:15 Miramar Beach → Panjim Market

19:30 Return to hotel, dinner & rest

Day 4 — 19 Nov (Checkout)

09:00 Breakfast @ Café Chocolatti → Quick beach walk

12:00 Checkout, transfer to airport

Packing + Essentials

Clothes & Beach

  • Light cotton shirts, shorts
  • Swimwear + extra towel
  • Slippers / sandals, sunglasses, cap

Essentials

  • Sunscreen (SPF 30–50)
  • Powerbank, phone waterproof pouch
  • Wet wipes, small backpack
  • Mosquito repellent, basic first-aid

Top Photo / Reel Spots

Key spots: Arambol Beach (sunset + drums), Fort Aguada, Chapora Fort cliff, Vagator cliff, Fontainhas (Latin Quarter), Dona Paula viewpoint.

Food & Shopping Guide

Food: Sample Goan prawn curry, king-fish thali, bebinca dessert.

Shopping:

  • Candolim / Calangute: beachwear, shell jewellery, flip-flops
  • Panjim Market: cashews, Goan spices, bebinca
  • Arambol: boho clothes, dreamcatchers, anklets

Transport & Tips

  • Use GoaMiles / QuickCab / FROGO for local travel.
  • Reapply sunscreen often; stay hydrated.
  • Avoid leaving valuables on beach chairs.
  • Wear comfy footwear; Goa has sandy + rocky parts.
